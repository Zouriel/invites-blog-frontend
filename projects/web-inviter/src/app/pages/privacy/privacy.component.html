Legal Privacy Policy How invites.blog handles the information you and your guests share with us.
What we collect

To send invitations we process the guest details you upload — names, and where provided, email addresses and phone numbers — along with the content of your invitation and your host contact details.

How we use it

Guest information is used solely to deliver your invitations, personalize each one, and report delivery and RSVP status back to you. We do not sell your data or your guests' data.

Retention

Campaign data is retained for the life of your campaign and a reasonable period afterward so you can access your dashboard. You can request deletion of a campaign and its guest list at any time.

Sharing

We share data only with the service providers needed to deliver invitations (for example, email and payment processors), and only to the extent required to provide the service.

Your choices

Guests can opt out of future messages for a campaign. Hosts can remove guests or cancel a campaign from the dashboard.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@invites.blog.
